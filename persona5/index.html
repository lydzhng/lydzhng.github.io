Story

In the game, Akira is taken in by a man of the name Sojiro. He attends Shujin Academy and meets Ryuji, who is running late to school. They walk to school together and become friends, but before they know it, they are brought into a palace. They meet their P.E teacher; Kamoshida. Kamoshida sends them to a cell and decides to kill Ryuji. Akira awakens his persona Arsene, saving Ryuji in the process. When they are found by the guards while escaping, Kamoshida reveals that he intentionally broke Ryuji's leg. Angered by such, Ryuji reveals his persona. He then goes back to the castle after escaping with the help of Arsene . He comes back to Kamoshida's palace while unintentionally bring Ann. She finds out about the secrets of Kamoshida within the palace and finds a clone of herself, but much more sexualized.
